Frequently Asked Questions

Moxie's

Quick Facts

Here are a few of the questions I most commonly receive as a therapist. If you have a question that is not included here, please don't hesitate to reach out! I would love to chat with you more about the process.

  • Do you take insurance?

    I am happy to say that I do accept insurance! I take Blue Cross Blue Shield as well as Medicaid. I also accept pay via American Express, Mastercard and Venmo if that is your personal preference.

  • Who do you work with?

    I am particularly drawn to working with folks ages 14-25, focusing on areas such as helping you understand your learning profiles, managing symptoms of overwhelm such as depression and anxiety, processing trauma and grief, as well as supporting social skills development.

  • Where are you located?

    My office is located in the heart of downtown Waterbury. I am walking distance to parks, coffee shops, and all the other quaint gems that the village has to offer. It makes for the perfect opportunity to take a session outside on nice days, or for my clients to grab a coffee or a bite to eat after their session while they are already in town!

  • What is your intake process like?

    As a therapist, I place great importance on the relationship between the therapist and the client. It's essential to feel comfortable and connected to your therapist. To ensure this connection, I offer a complimentary 15-minute phone consultation before beginning our sessions. After the consult, if it feels like a good fit, I work with my clients to find a time that works for us to connect, either remotely or in person on a weekly or bi-weekly basis.
